Global Data Protection Framework

1. Regulatory Compliance

CINEXA+ operates under the strict guidelines of the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA), the UK GDPR, and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We act as the Data Controller for all H.E.M. Group ecosystem nodes.

2. Information Collection

  • Identity: Name, email, and phone number via registration.
  • Telemetry: IP address, device type, and browser fingerprinting for security.
  • Viewing Data: Movie preferences and watch history (Compliant with US VPPA).

3. Video Privacy Protection (US/UK)

In accordance with the Video Privacy Protection Act (VPPA), your viewing history is encrypted and will not be disclosed to third-party advertisers without explicit digital consent, except for the purpose of personalizing your H.E.M. Group recommendations.

4. Your Rights (GDPR/NDPA)

Users have the right to:
• Request a copy of their data (Subject Access Request).
• Request "The Right to be Forgotten" (Full account/data deletion).
• Withdraw consent for automated profiling at any time.

5. Contact Data Protection Officer

For inquiries regarding H.E.M. Group data protocols: dpo@hem-group.ng
